About

I’m JoAnn Bishop, a student, advocate, researcher, and writer committed to protecting the overlooked and giving voice to stories that matter. My work blends criminal justice, advocacy, research, and storytelling with heart, purpose, and persistence.

Education

Southern New Hampshire University — Criminal Justice and Human Services academic focus.

Advocacy

My advocacy centers on children, families, victims, and those whose pain is too often minimized or ignored. I care deeply about connection, fairness, dignity, and emotional safety.

Research

My research interests include criminal justice systems, investigative analysis, victim-centered work, social issues, and the deeper truths that often sit beneath the surface.

Writing

Writing is one of the strongest threads running through everything I do. I write to advocate, to tell the truth, to connect, and to leave something meaningful behind.
